# NLP Exercises

I'm working through the online NLP textbook [Speech and Language Processing](https://web.stanford.edu/~jurafsky/slp3/) by Dan Jurafsky and James H. Martin. N.B. they've been through a change of edition since I started; I've done Chapters 1-4 from the 12 January 2025 draft before moving over to the 6 January 2026 draft and picking up at Chapter 4, which seems to correspond to Chapter 5 of the 12 January 2025 draft.

## Other files

- 1_John.txt: the first Epistle of John from the Bible (Authorized version), used for the n-gram exercises in Chapter 3 of the 2025 edition.
